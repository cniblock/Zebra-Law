Fraud

In the fight against fraud, we deploy a full suite of advanced investigative resources via our strategic partnerships. Our bespoke telematics systems enable us to seamlessly ingest and analyse a diverse array of fraud indicators, ensuring a thorough examination of potential risks. Moreover, we tailor our approach to align with our clients’ unique risk parameters, augmenting and refining their existing frameworks to bolster effectiveness.

By employing enhanced data analytics, sophisticated claims handling methodologies and expert investigative techniques, we are meticulous in our approach to tackling fraudulent activities throughout every facet of the MGA market.

Connected Vehicle / Automation

Within the realm of Connected Vehicles, our dedication lies in harnessing cutting-edge technology and data analytics. Our expertise spans telematics to comprehensive ECU interrogation where we have the capacity to gather diverse vehicle data tailored to specific makes and models. This enables Zebra to furnish our clients with actionable insights and strategic guidance, empowering them to make informed decisions and mitigate risks effectively. We are committed to significantly reducing traditional legal spend and claims expense by equipping clients with invaluable insights to proactively manage their risk profiles.

Moreover, in the realm of Automation, we possess market-leading expertise in autonomous vehicles, understanding the challenges faced by OEMs and insurers alike. With an extensive network of key stakeholders and industry contacts, we are well-equipped to adapt to and navigate future changes in the claims landscape stemming from automation and other technological advancements.

Catastrophic / Large Loss

When it comes to catastrophic and large loss claims, we recognise the profound impact such claims have on individuals and the wider claims community. We are fully alive to the importance of staying attuned to wider commercial realities and we provide our clients with unwavering support, expert guidance and sound economical advice throughout the life of these matters.

Through our market leading position and bespoke telematics platform, we advocate and adopt a “crisis-response” mindset; to act with compassion, resilience and empathy to mitigate tensions and reduce the overall impact of these events. We are uniquely positioned to make a genuine difference to all stakeholders by virtue of our technology and unique expertise. Our proactive approach and tailored solutions empower clients to navigate complex claims with speed, confidence and dignity.

Complex / Strategic

In the dynamic landscape of complex and strategic litigation, collaboration is not just a strategy; it’s our ethos. We understand our pivotal role in orchestrating the relevant expertise and proactively managing risks through our extensive network of partnerships and resources. With a profound understanding of legal intricacies and strategic acumen, we adeptly navigate multifaceted cases, ensuring every detail is addressed with precision and expertise.

We have a proven track record in undertaking “special projects” for our clients; ranging from targeted litigation to reduce an MGA’s overall expenditure to adopting a methodology and process for run-off binders in distress. Our ability to swiftly devise novel solutions to intricate issues faced by our clients in a wide variety of arenas sets us apart from the traditional legal market. We consistently conduct meticulous case analysis and collaborate with our clients to understand their specific challenge to deliver solutions to meet their needs in a cost-effective and reliable manner. At every step we fiercely champion our clients’ interests and safeguard their success in the arena of strategic litigation.
